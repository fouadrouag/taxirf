Privacy Policy

Introduction

Taxi RF is committed to protecting the confidentiality of your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data collected

When you make a booking or get in touch, we collect:

  • First and last name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Pick-up and drop-off addresses
  • Date and time of the trip
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe, not stored by Taxi RF)

Use of data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Confirm and organise your booking
  • Contact you if needed regarding your trip
  • Process your payment via Stripe
  • Comply with our legal obligations

Your data is never sold or shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

Retention period

Your data is kept for a maximum of 3 years from your last interaction, in accordance with the legal obligations applicable to transport service providers.

Cookies

This site uses only technical cookies necessary for the proper functioning of its services:

  • Google Maps: for the fare calculator and address autocomplete
  • Stripe: for secure payment processing
  • Google Places API: to display Google reviews

No advertising or tracking cookies are used on this site.

Your rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of your data
  • Right of rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: request the deletion of your data
  • Right to object: object to the processing of your data
  • Right to data portability: receive your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@taxirf.com

Security

Your data is protected by appropriate technical measures. Payments are processed exclusively by Stripe, PCI DSS certified, and Taxi RF does not store any banking data.
