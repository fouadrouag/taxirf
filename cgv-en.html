A deposit of 30% of the highest estimated amount is required at the time of online booking, by card via Stripe.

The balance is paid directly to the driver at the end of the trip, in cash or by card, based on the amount shown on the approved taxi meter.

Article 5 — Cancellation & Refunds

More than 24 hours before departure: free cancellation, deposit refunded 100%.

Between 4 and 24 hours before departure:

Less than 4 hours before departure: no refund.

These terms apply because a vehicle and driver are mobilised exclusively for your trip from the moment of confirmation, and other trip requests are declined in order to honour your booking.
